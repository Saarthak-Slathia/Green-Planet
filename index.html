Eco-System

What is This Eco-System ?

According to Wikipedia, "An ecosystem is a community of living organisms in conjunction with the nonliving components of their environment, interacting as a system. These biotic and abiotic components are linked together through nutrient cycles and energy flows."

This means that we all are in an eco-system and we depend on living as well as non-living things for survival and same also applies for other living and non-living things.

Saving The Environment

Here are some tips to save the environment, if you are asking why, click here

Plant a tree on any occassion

Plant atleast 1 tree on any special occassion like your birthday, any festival etc. Trees are important as they filter out the carbon-di-oxide that we breath out in the air to fresh pure oxygen which we breath in. They also provide us food, air, medecines and the list is endless.

Switch off devices when not in use.

Switching off the devices like fans, lights etc. helps in reducing electricity usage, not only your electricity bill ü§ë but as a fact that a large portion of the electricity is generated by burning coal and fossil fuels which leads to air pollution.

Don't Waste Water

Switch off the tap while bushing and don't use shower while bathing as there is a lot of water being wasted while you brush with the tap running on. Using a bucket and a mug while bathing can be useful as in showers the quantity of water being wasted is too much üíß

Waste in Dustbins

Throw wastes into dustbins, dry waste in blue one and wet waste in green, hazardous in red etc. This helps to reduce land pollution. Along with following this and throwing the waste in dustbins, also try to spread awareness amongg others to reduce pollution by the same.

Don't use plastic

Don't use plastic bags while going to shopping. Try to use paper straws and not plastic straws. As plastic is "non-biodegradable", ie - which cannot be decomposed, it harms the environment as it is too difficult to get rid of it. If we burn it, it releases poisonous and hazardous gases.

Spread Awareness

Spread Awareness among others like your friends, family members etc. to save the environment, not use plastic bags and throw waste in the appropriate dustbin, saving electricity, save water, don't burst crackers üß®üéá and plant a tree on your birthday.
